Our Story

Our passion is making you feel beautiful.

Tina is a D.C. native with over 15 years of experience in the industry.
After studying in New York, and working abroad in Paris and Milan, Tina decided to bring her
artistry and expertise back to her home.

With the goal of being the top hair salon in D.C. Tina work to not only provide her customers with the best services but to give back to the community she loves.
